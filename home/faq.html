Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a car?

You can book a car by selecting your preferred dates on the homepage, browsing available cars, and confirming your booking.

Do I need an account to book?

Yes, you need to create an account and log in before making a booking.

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, bookings can be cancelled before the scheduled time through your dashboard.

Who can use this service?

The service is available to students, staff, and faculty members of MUST.

Is the service available 24/7?

Availability depends on campus transport schedules, but bookings can be made anytime.
